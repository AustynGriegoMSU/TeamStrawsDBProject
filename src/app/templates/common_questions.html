Transfers

Why do I need to choose a source account?
Transfers require you to pick which account to debit before submitting.

Can I transfer to the same account?
No. Transfers must go to a different recipient account number.

Junior / Youth Accounts

Can I set up a parental-control account for my child?
Yes. {{ call_or_visit_branch }} to request a youth account for your child. A parent or guardian must be the primary account holder and co-signer. Once set up, the child's account will have limited visibility — balance and transaction history are accessible, but sensitive personal and account details are restricted.

What restrictions apply to a youth account?
Spending is capped at a parent-approved daily limit, outgoing transfers require guardian approval, and account settings cannot be changed by the child. This gives kids hands-on experience managing real money in a controlled, safe environment.

How do I update or remove the parental controls?
{{ call_or_visit_branch }}. The parent or guardian on file must verify their identity before any changes to youth-account restrictions can be made.
